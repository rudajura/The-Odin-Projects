Scrambled eggs with bacon

Why should you prepare this?

Are you tired of sweet breakfasts? Why not to prepare healthy and fast meal, that will feed you and bring you a lot of proteins and healthy fats?

Scrambled eggs are popular all over the world, and you can make them in many ways, so they never get boring.

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 4 spices of bacon
  • A pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Take a pan, put butter onto pan and turn the heat to medium-low.
  2. Once the butter is melted and foaming, add all slices of bacon there and fry it.
  3. Meanwhile whisk all eggs inside some cup.
  4. When the bacon is already fried, insert it onto plate.
  5. Pour eggs from cup onto a pan and constantly mix them with wooden spoon until they are fully fried.
  6. Put those eggs onto plate, salt them.
  7. You are done! You can serve it with some bread or vegetables.
